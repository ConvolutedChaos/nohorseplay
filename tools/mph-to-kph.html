MPH to KPH Converter


MPH (Miles per hour) is a unit of speed commonly used in the United States.
KPH (Kilometers per hour) is a unit of speed commonly used in most other countries, like Europe.

To get KPH, multiply your MPH value by 1.60934.

Enter a speed in miles per hour, and then click the button to convert it to kilometers per hour.
