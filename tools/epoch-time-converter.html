Epoch Time Converter

But first, a little context...

Epoch time (also called Unix time or POSIX time) is a way computers count time.

  • It started on January 1, 1970, at midnight (UTC).
  • Time is measured in seconds since that moment.
  • For example:
  • 0 = January 1, 1970, 00:00:00 UTC
  • 1,000,000,000 = September 9, 2001, 01:46:40 UTC
  • 1,700,000,000 = December 14, 2023, 17:46:40 UTC
  • But, on January 19, 2038, at 03:14:08 UTC, the epoch time will reach its maximum value and reset to 0.
