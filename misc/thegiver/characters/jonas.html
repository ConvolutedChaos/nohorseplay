Jonas is the protaganist of the book The Giver. Among his family members are his mom, dad, and his little sister named Lily. Jonas is selceted rather than assigned like the other Twelves. In his folder that all Twelves get when they get assigned, he only has a single piece of paper for his rules unlike the other Twelves, who get many sheets of paper of rules. Jonas goes to the Annex to meet with The Giver. In his first two meetings, he recieves the memories of snow, sunlight, and a sunburn. At first, he enjoys being the Reciever of Memory, but after he experiences the sunburn, he knows that there will be some more pain. His two best friends are Fiona and Asher, who are both Twelves and get assigned with Jonas.
