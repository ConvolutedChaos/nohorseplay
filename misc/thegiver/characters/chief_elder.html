The Chief Elder is one of the most important charactors in the Giver. She is the person is resonsible for making speeches and assigning the Twelves. She is the head of the entire community. She belives that strict rules and "Sameness" are nessesary to eliminate pain and suffering. She is the one who assigns Twelves their roles and makes important speeches that have to do with various parts of the community. With her and the Elders, they selected Jonas instead of assigning him a regular role. They chose him to be Receiver of Memory.
